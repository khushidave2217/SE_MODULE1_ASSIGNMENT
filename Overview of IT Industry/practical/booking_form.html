Beauty Parlor Booking System

Case Study: Functionality of Three Layers

Presentation Layer

  • User Interface - Booking form with inputs for Name, Service, Date
  • Displays available services
  • Shows confirmation or error messages

Business Logic Layer

  • Validates booking information
  • Checks availability of services
  • Manages booking and payment status
  • Handles payment approval process

Data Access Layer

  • Stores booking and payment data
  • Retrieves service information
  • Updates payment status
